Warframe repository

Introduction:

Warframe drops you into a far‑future solar system where ancient warriors called Tenno awaken from centuries of sleep and find themselves in the middle of a sprawling interplanetary conflict. You play as one of these Tenno, piloting biomechanical suits known as Warframes—each with its own unique abilities, combat style, and personality. The game blends fast, acrobatic movement with cooperative missions, deep customization, and an ever‑expanding storyline that mixes sci‑fi mystery, space‑opera drama, and stylish action. It's a world built to evolve, constantly adding new quests, characters, and planets to explore.

About warframe:

    • Release & Developer: Warframe launched in 2013 and is developed by Digital Extremes, a studio known for supporting the game with constant updates and community‑driven events.
    • Genre & Style: It's a free‑to‑play, fast‑paced sci‑fi action game built around acrobatics, gunplay, melee combat, and deep customization. Warframes: There are dozens of Warframes, each acting like a unique character class with its own abilities, strengths, and playstyle.
    • Setting: The story unfolds across a reimagined solar system filled with factions like the Grineer, Corpus, Infested, and Sentients.
    • Gameplay Loop: Players complete missions, craft gear, level up equipment, and uncover lore through cinematic quests.
    • Community & Updates: Warframe is known for its active community and frequent expansions that add new planets, open‑world zones, quests, and mechanics.
